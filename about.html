FounderCalculators was built to bridge the gap between "gut feeling" and data-driven startup management through professional-grade modeling tools.

Our Mission

The startup world is filled with complex jargon and "black box" metrics. Our mission is to democratize financial literacy for entrepreneurs. We provide free, transparent, and easy-to-use SaaS and startup financial tools that help founders model their runway, value their companies, and understand their unit economics with professional clarity.

Why Trust Our Tools?

Every calculator on this site is benchmarked against modern industry standards and SaaS founder best practices. We don't just provide the formula; we provide the context.

  • ✔ Expert-Backed: Developed by operators with experience in both bootstrapped and venture-backed environments.
  • ✔ Always Current: We update our valuation multiples and unit economic assumptions to reflect the modern startup ecosystem.
  • ✔ Privacy First: Your data is processed locally in your browser. We do not store or transmit your sensitive business inputs.

Our Methodology

We believe in transparency. Unlike many black-box platforms, we explain the how and why behind every calculation. Whether it's the Rule of 40 for SaaS efficiency or the Berkus Method for pre-revenue valuation, we use widely accepted frameworks utilized by venture capitalists and angel investors globally.
