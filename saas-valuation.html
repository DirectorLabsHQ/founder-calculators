How SaaS Companies are Valued in 2026

In 2026, the SaaS valuation landscape has shifted away from "growth at any cost." While revenue growth remains the primary lever, **capital efficiency** and **Net Revenue Retention (NRR)** have become equal partners in determining your multiple.

The Three Pillars of Your Multiple

  1. Growth Rate: The "velocity" of your business. Triple-Triple-Double-Double-Double remains the gold standard for reaching unicorn status.
  2. Net Revenue Retention (NRR): Measures how much your existing customers grow. In 2026, an NRR of 120%+ is the single best way to secure a 15x+ multiple.
  3. Gross Margin: High-margin software (80%+) is valued significantly higher than tech-enabled services (30-50%) because it scales without proportional costs.
