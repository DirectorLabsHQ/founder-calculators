Understanding SaaS Valuation Multiples

Valuing a SaaS business is primarily driven by its revenue quality and growth trajectory. In 2026, healthy early-stage SaaS startups typically command multiples between 6x and 12x ARR, though "outliers" with 100%+ growth can still see higher premiums.

The Valuation Formula

Our calculator applies a baseline multiple and adjusts it based on your growth rate:

  • Baseline: 5x ARR for stable growth (20-40%).
  • Growth Bonus: Additional 1x-5x added for high-growth tiers (50% to 200%+).
