Gross Margin Benchmarks by Industry – Realistic 2026 Numbers

Gross margin is the single biggest predictor of long-term profitability — but every industry has different rules. Here's what realistic numbers look like in 2026.

Written by operators who’ve run businesses across SaaS, agencies, e-commerce, and marketplaces.

Why gross margin matters more than revenue

Gross margin = (Revenue – Cost of Goods Sold) / Revenue

COGS includes only direct costs to serve customers:

  • Hosting & infrastructure
  • Payment processing fees
  • Customer support (when direct to client)
  • Contractor/freelancer delivery costs
  • Other direct costs to serve customers

As businesses scale, hosting costs per user fall, but enterprise support costs rise — margins may shift up or down depending on how you manage delivery.

Low gross margin can turn explosive revenue growth into negative profits.
