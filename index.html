Why We Built FounderCalculators

Most startup founders fail because they run out of cash or build something people don't want. In the current venture landscape, investors are looking for capital efficiency, strong unit economics, and a clear path to profitability.

FounderCalculators was created by founders and operators who have built and analyzed SaaS businesses across multiple stages, from bootstrapped startups to venture-backed companies.

How to use these tools:

  • Fundraising: Use the Equity Dilution and Berkus Method tools to prepare for seed or Series A rounds.
  • Growth: Monitor your Viral Coefficient to see if your product-led growth is working.
  • Operations: Calculate your Burdened Employee Cost before hiring your next team members.
