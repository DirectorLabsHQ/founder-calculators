Unlike generic finance tools, our calculators are benchmarked against modern startup metrics including SaaS valuation multiples, CAC-to-LTV ratios, burn rate runway standards, and unit economics used by investors in 2026.

Use these tools for pitch preparation, internal planning, or stress-testing business assumptions before making high-stakes decisions.
