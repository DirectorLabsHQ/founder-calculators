What is a Good ROAS in 2026?

ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) measures how much gross revenue you earn for every dollar spent on advertising. For most venture-backed startups, a 4:1 ROAS is considered a healthy baseline for scaling paid acquisition channels.

The ROAS Formula

ROAS = Total Revenue from Ads / Total Ad Spend
